Features

Everything you need.
Nothing you don't.

Triply does one thing really well — turns the travel content you already watch into actionable plans.

Reel to Bucket List

Paste an Instagram Reel URL or share directly from Instagram. Triply watches it and extracts every travel destination mentioned.

Every Place, One Map

All extracted locations plotted on an interactive map. See the full picture of every destination from a single reel at a glance.

Collections

Organize your saved places into collections — by trip, by vibe, by dream. Your own travel library, always growing.

AI-Powered Extraction

Powered by AI that understands context — not just place names, but the actual locations shown in the video content.

How it works

Three steps. That's it.

01

Share a Reel

See a travel reel you love? Hit share and send it to Triply, or paste the URL directly in the app.

02

Triply extracts every place

AI identifies all locations in the reel and pins them on your map. Each place gets its own card with details.

03

Save, organize, explore

Add places to your bucket list, create collections, and start planning. Your travel inspiration — finally organized.

Stop losing travel inspo.
