Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2, 2026 · Effective: February 19, 2026

Aucto AI ("we," "us," or "our") operates the Triply mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, share, and protect your information when you use the App, and the choices you have regarding your data.

This policy applies to all users of the App worldwide. Certain sections apply specifically to users in the European Economic Area ("EEA"), United Kingdom, and California, as noted.

If you do not agree with this Privacy Policy, please do not use the App.

2. How We Use Your Information

We process your information for the following purposes only:

  • Providing the Service: To authenticate your account, store your curations, and enable core features.
  • Personalization: To display your name within the App.
  • AI-Powered Features: The App uses automated processing to extract place information from publicly available Instagram Reel and TikTok content. This processing occurs on our servers and does not use your personal data for training AI models.
  • Analytics: To understand aggregate usage patterns and improve the App. Analytics data is anonymous.
  • Diagnostics: To identify, diagnose, and resolve crashes and performance issues.
  • Security: To detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and unauthorized access.

We do not use your information for:

  • Advertising or ad targeting
  • Selling data to third parties
  • Profiling for automated decision-making that produces legal or similarly significant effects
  • Training machine learning models on your personal data

5. Data Storage, Security, and Transfers

5.1 Storage

Your account data is stored on secure cloud servers provided by Supabase. Certain preferences and device identifiers are stored locally on your device using encrypted storage (Keychain on iOS, Keystore on Android) and are not transmitted to our servers.

5.2 Security Measures

We implement industry-standard technical and organizational measures to protect your data, including:

  • Encryption in transit (TLS/HTTPS) and at rest
  • Row-level security policies on database access
  • Secure authentication via OAuth 2.0 (Google, Apple)
  • Locally encrypted storage for device-level data

No method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security. In the unlikely event of a data breach affecting your personal information, we will notify you and the relevant authorities as required by applicable law.

5.3 International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and processed on servers located outside your country of residence, including the United States. Where data is transferred outside the EEA/UK, we ensure appropriate safeguards are in place, such as Standard Contractual Clauses approved by the European Commission.

6. Data Retention

  • Account data (name, email): Retained while your account is active. Deleted within 30 days of account deletion.
  • Curations and saved places: Retained while your account is active. Deleted within 30 days of account deletion.
  • Anonymous analytics data: May be retained indefinitely in aggregate, non-identifiable form.
  • Crash and diagnostic data: Retained for up to 90 days, then automatically deleted.

We do not retain personal data longer than necessary for the purposes described in this policy.

7. Your Rights and Choices

7.1 All Users

Regardless of your location, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Delete your account and associated personal data
  • Opt out of analytics data collection

Account Deletion: You can delete your account directly from within the App. Upon deletion, all personal data is removed within 30 days. To request account deletion via email, contact connect@aucto.ai.

7.2 Additional Rights for EEA/UK Users (GDPR)

Under the GDPR, you additionally have the right to:

  • Data Portability: Receive your data in a structured, commonly used, machine-readable format
  • Restrict Processing: Request limitation of processing in certain circumstances
  • Object to Processing: Object to processing based on legitimate interests
  • Withdraw Consent: Where processing is based on consent, withdraw it at any time without affecting prior processing
  • Lodge a Complaint: File a complaint with your local data protection supervisory authority

7.3 Additional Rights for California Residents (CCPA/CPRA)

Under the California Consumer Privacy Act and the California Privacy Rights Act, California residents have the right to:

  • Know what personal information is collected and how it is used
  • Delete personal information held by us
  • Opt out of the sale of personal information (we do not sell your data)
  • Non-discrimination for exercising your privacy rights

Categories of personal information collected (as defined by the CCPA): Identifiers (name, email). We do not collect commercial information, biometric data, geolocation, or internet browsing history.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at connect@aucto.ai. We will verify your identity and respond within 30 days (or 45 days if an extension is necessary, with notice).

8. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13 (or 16 in the EEA/UK, or the applicable age of consent in your jurisdiction).

If we become aware that we have collected personal data from a child without verified parental consent, we will take steps to delete that information promptly.

If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us immediately at connect@aucto.ai.

10. AI and Automated Processing Disclosure

The App uses artificial intelligence to extract place information from publicly available Instagram Reel and TikTok URLs that you provide. This processing:

  • Does not make decisions that produce legal or similarly significant effects on you
  • Does not use your personal data to train or improve AI models
  • Processes data on secure servers with results stored in your account

11. Do Not Track Signals

The App does not respond to "Do Not Track" browser signals, as there is no industry-standard for mobile applications. However, you may opt out of analytics collection by contacting us.
